# 🤖 My AI Companion — Native Android App

A floating AI bubble that **lives on your home screen**, moves up and down,
talks to you, asks how you're doing, and keeps running **even when your phone is locked**.

This is a REAL native Android app (not a website), so it can do what a website cannot:
- ✅ Float over your home screen and other apps
- ✅ Keep running when the phone is locked
- ✅ Talk to you out loud (text-to-speech)
- ✅ Ask you questions on a timer
- ✅ Restart automatically after you reboot your phone

---

## 📲 HOW TO GET THE APP ON YOUR PHONE

You do NOT need a computer or Android Studio. GitHub builds the app for you.

### Step 1 — Wait for the build
After this code is pushed, GitHub automatically builds the app.
1. Go to your repo on github.com
2. Click the **"Actions"** tab at the top
3. Wait for the green ✅ checkmark (takes ~3-5 minutes)

### Step 2 — Download the app file (APK)
1. Click the finished build (green ✅)
2. Scroll down to **"Artifacts"**
3. Tap **"MyAICompanion-APK"** to download it
4. It downloads a .zip — open it to get **app-debug.apk**

### Step 3 — Install on your phone
1. Open the **app-debug.apk** file on your phone
2. Android will say "install blocked" — tap **Settings → Allow from this source**
3. Tap **Install**

### Step 4 — Set it up
1. Open the **My AI Companion** app
2. Type your name, the AI's name, and your **Groq API key** (free from console.groq.com)
3. Tap **💾 Save Settings**
4. Tap **1️⃣ Allow Float Permission** → turn ON "Display over other apps"
5. Tap **2️⃣ START MY AI 🚀**
6. Your AI bubble appears and starts floating! Minimize the app.

---

## 🎮 How to use it
- **Drag** the bubble anywhere with your finger
- **Tap** the bubble to open the full chat
- It **floats up and down** on its own
- It **talks** and **asks questions** every few minutes
- It keeps going **when your screen is locked**

## ⛔ To stop it
Open the app → tap **⛔ Stop AI**
